Louisi AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2024

At Louisi AI, protecting your personal data is our top priority. This privacy policy is designed to inform you about the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it secure. By using our AI-generated image sharing platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect various types of information when you use Louisi AI:

a. Information Provided Voluntarily

- Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect details such as your name, email address, and any other data you choose to share with us.
- Content: Images you upload, generate, or share on the platform.

b. Automatically Collected Information

- Usage Data: We gather information about your activity on the site, such as pages visited, session duration, and actions performed (e.g., uploading or sharing images).
- Technical Data: Your IP address, browser type, device used, and operating system may be automatically collected.

2. Data Usage

We use the data collected for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To allow you to access our services, share, and generate images via AI.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze how the platform is used and identify potential improvements.
  • Communications: To respond to your inquiries, inform you of important updates, or send notifications related to the use of the site.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and regulations.

3. Data Sharing

We are committed to not selling or renting your personal data to third parties. However, your data may be shared in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, maintenance, etc.) under contracts that ensure the security of your information.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data if required by law, a court order, or as part of a legal investigation.

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect data to enhance your user experience. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser, but this may affect certain functionalities of the site.

6. User Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Objection: You may object to the use of your personal data for specific purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the following email address: louisi.contact@icloud.com.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.
